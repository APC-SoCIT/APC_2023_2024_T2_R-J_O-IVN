Product Inventory

Designed to help you effortlessly manage and track the availability of your products. It allows you to keep an up-to-date record of your stock, monitor restocking needs, and avoid overstock or stockout situations.

With real-time insights, you can maintain optimal inventory levels and ensure that your customers always have access to the products they desire.

Sales Report

The Sales Report feature provides a comprehensive overview of your business's sales performance. It includes detailed analytics, allowing you to assess sales trends, identify top-selling products, and understand customer purchasing behavior.

By generating insightful reports, this feature empowers you to make informed decisions, optimize marketing strategies, and drive business growth.

Fresh Selection

The Fresh Selection feature showcases your menu of fresh products, presenting a visually appealing and organized display of the items you offer. It allows you to easily update and customize your menu, ensuring that your customers are informed about the latest and freshest selections available.

Customer Orders

It provides a user-friendly interface for customers to browse your menu, select items, and complete the ordering process seamlessly. This feature also helps you manage and fulfill customer orders with accuracy and promptness, contributing to overall customer satisfaction.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this only available for Villamor Air Base?

    Currently, our application is exclusively available to residents of Villamor Air Base, Pasay City. We are actively exploring opportunities for expansion to other areas in the future.

  • How long will it take my vegetables and/or fruits will deliver?

    n terms of the delivery process, our team is committed to providing a prompt and efficient service. Typically, the delivery process takes a minimum of minutes to a maximum of 3 hours from the time your order is confirmed. This timeframe is designed to ensure that you receive the freshest produce possible. We understand the importance of timely and reliable delivery, and our team works diligently to meet or exceed your expectations. Once your order is processed, you will receive a notification with an estimated delivery time.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    At this time, we only accept cash payments upon delivery. Our delivery personnel, who is also the owner of the business, will collect the payment in cash during the delivery process. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in making cash payments for your orders. If you have any concerns or special requests regarding payment, please feel free to contact us, and we will do our best to assist you.

  • Are the product images on your website accurate representations of the actual items?

    We strive to provide accurate and high-quality product images. However, please note that there may be slight variations due to factors such as monitor settings and lighting conditions.
